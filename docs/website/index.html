Verendrye: Dumoine River 2018

This past summer, I went on a 17 day canoe trip with my camp in Verendrye park. This park is in northern Quebec. During this trip, myself and a group of five other campers (accompanied by two staff) had to live in the wild. During this trip, we would usually leave camp around six, paddle and portage (carry the boats and other stuff) for eight hours, then get back to camp. We all were given a designated role back at camp. This trip was a life changing experience for me and was a lot of fun. Above, in the menu bar, you can click the names of the campers and staff to go to a photo of them. Enjoy!
